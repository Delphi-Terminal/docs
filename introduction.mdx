The Delphi Terminal API gives you unified access to prediction market data across multiple exchanges — **Kalshi (KLSI)**, **Polymarket (POLY)**, **Limitless**, and **Pfun** — through a single, consistent interface.

## Supported exchanges

| Exchange | ID Prefix | Description |
|----------|-----------|-------------|
| Kalshi | `klsi_id` | US-regulated prediction market exchange |
| Polymarket | `poly_id` | Decentralized prediction market platform |
| Limitless | `market_id` | Prediction market platform |
| Pfun | `market_id` | Prediction market platform |
